ABOUT ME

Ji Seon Oh

UX/UI Designer

I am an enthusiastic designer and a third-year student studying design in Interactive Arts and Technology School at Simon Fraser University. I have always been interested in and passionate about finding and solving design problems around us. As I have been working on numerous design projects such as mobile app development and web design development based on user experiences, I have learned about the importance of user experience. User experience is essential for the design to make more profitable and attractive. Every time I design something, considering the user experiences is the first step that I do. As a user experience designer, both aesthetics and usability are very important values to me.

By accomplishing the projects, I am professional in Adobe illustrator and Adobe Photoshop. Also, I am familiar with HTML, CSS and java languges by creating websites and games/applications. I have some experience in filming by using Adobe Premier Pro and Adobe After Effects.
